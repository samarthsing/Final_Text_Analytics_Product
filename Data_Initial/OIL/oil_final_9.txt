Tex Waco is the Texas oil billionaire that heads up a group of rich Texas vigilantes known as the Texas Twelve. A former business partner of Tex is kidnapped and held for ransom by a Mexican drug cartel in Oil Field, Texas. Tex and his two adopted sons, Kale and Ellis Baylock, and The General fly to Oil Field and bein to make their plans to bring the drug cartel to justice and take the cartel's money. Tex enlists the help of two other experienced operatives, Carlos and Bull Dog, and the vigilantes give the drug cartel a dose of Forty-Five Caliber Texas Justice.
